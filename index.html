How to actually use this

The move

Every card above is one HEB checkout. Clip all the coupons in a card (tap each chip), add items that satisfy each coupon's dollar threshold, and every coupon fires on the same basket. Items marked 2× or 3× count toward that many basket coupons at once — those are the ones doing the heavy lifting.

Bonus coupons (the green ＋ section) are item coupons — like "$6 off 2 AXE" — that apply to products already in the basket. Buy those items and the item coupon fires on top of the basket coupons. That's the difference between the basket total and the bigger "loaded" total on each card. Products with a green +coupon tag in the basket are the ones that carry one.

The sample basket is just one cheapest way to reach the thresholds — swap in the specific products you actually want (open the coupon to see all eligible items). As long as your spend in each coupon's product set clears its threshold, the discount applies.

Bonus values for percent-off and buy-more coupons are estimates from the item's price, so treat the loaded total as a close ballpark, not a guarantee. Standalone basket coupons in the table don't stack with another basket coupon, but they still carry their own bonus item coupons.
