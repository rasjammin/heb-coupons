How to actually use this

The move

Every card above is one HEB checkout. Clip all the coupons in a card (tap each chip), add items that satisfy each coupon's dollar threshold, and every coupon fires on the same basket. Items marked 2× or 3× count toward that many coupons at once — those are the ones doing the heavy lifting.

The sample basket is just one cheapest way to reach the thresholds — swap in the specific products you actually want (open the coupon to see all eligible items). As long as your spend in each coupon's product set clears its threshold, the discount applies.

Standalone coupons in the reference table don't stack with another basket coupon, but they're still good on their own and can layer item-level coupons on top.
